Project Showcase

Market Regime Detector

A model to detect market regimes based on market data to inform trading strategies, then turn the output into visuals that are easy to understand on a portfolio.

Project Overview

This project pulls market data, classifies the environment, and labels each period into a regime that can support trading analysis and strategy design. The Idea is to help during options trading to get the bias for the day and trade in that direction as a trend day and avoid chop days that burn theta.

  • Calm Bull Trend
  • Volatile Bull
  • Panic Bear
  • Calm Bear Trend
  • Low-Vol Chop
  • High-Vol Chop
  • Transition
View Script

Terminal And Results Preview

The script fetches price data, computes moving averages, realized volatility, momentum, and ATR %, then maps each date into a market regime and saves chart outputs.

$ python MarketRegimeDetector.py --symbol SPY --period 2y Latest regime summary Trend: Bullish Volatility: High Momentum: Positive Market Regime: Volatile Bull Saved labeled price chart to: spy_regime_chart.png Saved regime timeline chart to: spy_regime_timeline.png

Labeled Price Chart

This chart overlays SPY price with the 50 DMA and 200 DMA, then labels regime changes directly on the chart for quick visual interpretation.

Regime Timeline

The timeline view makes it easier to see when the market shifts from chop to trend, or from calm to high-volatility behavior across the full sample period.
